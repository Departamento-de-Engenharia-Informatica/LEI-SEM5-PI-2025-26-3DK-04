# US2.2.10 - View Status of Submited Vessel Visit Notifications

## 1. Requirements

### 1.1. User Story

**As a Shipping Agent Representative,** 
I want to view the status of all my submitted Vessel Visit Notifications (in progress, pending, approved with current dock assignment, or rejected with reason), so that I am always informed about the decisions of the Port Authority.

### 1.3. Acceptance Criteria

- The Shipping Agent Representative may also view the status of Vessel Visit Notifications submitted by other representatives working for the same shipping agent organization.
- Vessel Visit Notifications must be searchable and filterable by vessel, status, representative and time.
